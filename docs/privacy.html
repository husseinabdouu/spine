Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · This page is a placeholder — full policy coming soon.

What we collect

Spine collects biometric data (heart rate variability, sleep, activity, stress) from Apple Health and financial transaction data via Plaid. We collect only what is necessary to generate your Spine Score.

How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to compute your personalized Spine Score and surface insights within the app. We do not sell your data, share it with third parties, or use it to train AI models.

Data storage

Financial and biometric data is stored with row-level security via Supabase. Health data is processed on-device where possible. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Your rights

You may request deletion of all your data at any time by contacting us. We will comply within 30 days.

Contact

For privacy questions, reach us at privacy@joinspine.io.
